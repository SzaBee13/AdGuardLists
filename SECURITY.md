# Security

## Disclaimer

Only use our block/unblock lists if you know what you are doing. We are not responsible for any damage caused by using our lists. Use at your own risk.

## Reporting Vulnerabilities

If you find a vulnerability in our lists, please report it to us immediately. We will investigate the issue and take appropriate action to fix it. You can report vulnerabilities by opening an issue on our GitHub repository or by contacting us directly.

- [GitHub](https://github.com/SzaBee13/AdGuardLists/security/advisories/new)
- [Email](mailto:security@szabee.me)
- [Discord](https://s.szabee.me/dc)

## Responsible Disclosure

We follow responsible disclosure practices. We will not take legal action against researchers who report vulnerabilities in good faith. However, we reserve the right to take legal action against anyone who exploits vulnerabilities in our lists for malicious purposes. We encourage researchers to report vulnerabilities to us as soon as possible so that we can fix them before they are exploited by malicious actors.

## Security Updates

We will release security updates for our lists as soon as possible after a vulnerability is reported and fixed. We will also provide information about the vulnerability and the fix in the release notes for the update. We recommend that users keep their block/unblock lists up to date to ensure that they are protected against known vulnerabilities.

## Conclusion

We take security seriously and are committed to providing our users with safe and secure block/unblock lists. If you have any questions or concerns about the security of our lists, please do not hesitate to contact us.
